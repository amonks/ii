# 2001

## Sikesville Briefing

### Summary
LOTTIE convinces SEAMUS and ERASTUS at the HOLIDAY INN EXPRESS to storm CORNUCOPIA HOUSE under the NOC directive. The BURNT HELLO KITTY FOLDER and POLAROIDS establish the moral trigger and expose FAMILIES WITHOUT FRONTIERS.

#### Characters

- SEAMUS
  - Physician Seamus McGillicutty who signs onto the assault after seeing the evidence.
- ERASTUS
  - Veteran trucker Erastus Boudreau bringing transport and muscle to the briefing.

#### Objects
- BURNT HELLO KITTY FOLDER

- POLAROIDS
  - Disturbing photos inside the Burnt Hello Kitty Folder.

#### Locations
- HOLIDAY INN EXPRESS

- SIKESVILLE MD
  - Town hosting the professional development cover event and staging point.

## Cornucopia House

### Summary
The team infiltrates the HOUSE, BARN, SHED, and COTTAGE, confronting BEARD MAN, BLONDE MAN, and the OLD WOMAN. Evidence including the SMALL BLACK BOOK, BIG GREY SACK, and VHS TAPES documents the abuse before the structures burn. Posters of HULK HOGAN, JACKSON, and KEN GRIFFEY JR hint at curated normalcy for the children.

### Grounds and Structures

#### Locations
- CORNUCOPIA HOUSE
  - Rural foster compound near ZION MD targeted for the midnight assault.
- HOUSE
  - Main residence with boarded windows, hidden bunkrooms, and filthy common rooms.
- BARN
  - Former dairy structure turned cage stack for neglected animals and kids' pets.
- SHED
  - Outbuilding stuffed with VHS TAPES, recording gear, and the SMALL BLACK BOOK.
- COTTAGE
  - Cozy façade concealing the OLD WOMAN and the slug horror that ends in fire.
- BATHROOM

- FAMILY ROOM
  - Common area with mattresses and non-stop lurid television programming.
- THE CLEARING
  - Wooded opening holding every structure and providing cover for surveillance.
