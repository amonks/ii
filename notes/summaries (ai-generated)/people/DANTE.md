# DANTE

## Basic Info
- Son of LOTTIE and EDDIE
- Father to a child with CARMELLA
- Used to steal cars (per LOTTIE)

## Dialog

No direct dialog recorded.

## Events and Notes

### 2001
- EDDIE is expecting LOTTIE home to make dinner for DANTE and "some broad" (presumably CARMELLA)
- LOTTIE does not come home as expected due to Cornucopia house operation
- DANTE used to steal cars (mentioned by LOTTIE when discussing stealing a car for the operation)
- Has a child with CARMELLA (LOTTIE and EDDIE's grandchild)

### Threats from EDDIE
- After the operation, EDDIE threatens to bring DANTE and move to New Jersey with his family
- LOTTIE points out that CARMELLA is the mother of their grandchild and EDDIE can't just move her to a new state
