# LADYSLUG

The true form of KALAMATIANO (Yelena Kalamatiano). See KALAMATIANO for full details.

## Appearance

From Session 1-2:

> where there was an old woman, there is now a grey mass of flesh covered in abscesses, her face is just a lolling tongue and swollen-shut eyes, she's holding her tumorous udders, which are dripping a white slime, which we'd previously perceived as tea cups

> no legs, "big stinky milky slug"

> LADYSLUG follows us out the door; squishes through it like an octopus, stinks like rotting meat

## Combat

From Session 1-2:

> lady shoots her big black tongue out of her mouth at LOTTIE in a real frog move; LOTTIE dodges, tongue splats on the ground with an incredibly loud thwack like a moose hitting a car

Damage taken:
- LOTTIE attacks, does 1 damage
- ERASTUS shoots for 6 damage
- LOTTIE crits with shotgun for 11 damage, hits her in the face

## Death

From Session 1-2:

> ERASTUS lights a zippo and tosses it in the house, the LADYSLUG and house go up in flames
> LADYSLUG doesn't like this at all
