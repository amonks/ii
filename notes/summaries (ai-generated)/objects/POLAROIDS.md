# POLAROIDS

Photographs of child abuse found in the burnt Hello Kitty folder that LOTTIE brought to the briefing. The contents motivated the agents to fully commit to the massacre at CORNUCOPIA HOUSE.

>       - see below

## The Contents

## Effect on Agents

- ERASTUS lost 1 SAN and had intrusive violent fantasies about killing those responsible
- The POLAROIDS were compelling enough to make the agents commit to killing all adults at CORNUCOPIA HOUSE
- The player (of ERASTUS) was not shown the actual content, only the character's reaction
- The folder being burnt suggests it was recovered from a fire or deliberately damaged

## Connection to VHS TAPES

The POLAROIDS likely documented the same abuse that was being recorded on the VHS TAPES found in the shed at CORNUCOPIA HOUSE, suggesting a commercial child abuse operation.
