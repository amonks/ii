# 2001

## Sikesville Briefing - February 3, 2001

### Summary
On February 3, 2001, LOTTIE convinces SEAMUS and ERASTUS at the HOLIDAY INN EXPRESS in Sikesville to storm CORNUCOPIA HOUSE under the NOC directive during a domestic abuse seminar cover event.
The BURNT HELLO KITTY FOLDER and POLAROIDS establish the moral trigger and expose FAMILIES WITHOUT FRONTIERS.

### Holiday Inn Conference

#### Characters
- LOTTIE
  - DEA agent Charlotte Watkins who assembles the team and lays out the Plan in the seminar room, hobbyist graverobber.
- SEAMUS
  - Physician Seamus McGillicutty who signs onto the assault after seeing the evidence.
- ERASTUS
  - Veteran cardiologist Erastus Boudreau with his pickup truck, providing transport and muscle.

#### Objects
- BURNT HELLO KITTY FOLDER
  - Charred pink folder of Polaroids that locks LOTTIE into the mission.
- POLAROIDS
  - Disturbing photos inside the Burnt Hello Kitty Folder.
- NORMAL MANILLA FOLDER
  - Packet holding dossiers and FAMILIES WITHOUT FRONTIERS paperwork.
- FAMILIES WITHOUT FRONTIERS
  - Nonprofit shell tied to the Cornucopia tax filings.
- NOC
  - No Official Cover status requiring zero witnesses or evidence.

#### Locations
- HOLIDAY INN EXPRESS
  - Sikesville hotel where the agents pose as seminar attendees.
- SIKESVILLE MD
  - Town hosting the professional development cover event and staging point.

## Prep and Approach

### Summary
The team outfits themselves at the ROSARYVILLE ANNUAL GUN SHOW and packs ERASTUS's TRUCK with improvised gear.
A raccoon ambush, the BIG CROW, and the FURRY THING escalate omens while LOTTIE and SEAMUS steal the DIFFERENT MINIVAN in ZION MD.

## Cornucopia House

### Summary
The team infiltrates the HOUSE, BARN, SHED, and COTTAGE, confronting BEARD MAN, BLONDE MAN, and the OLD WOMAN.
Evidence including the SMALL BLACK BOOK, BIG GREY SACK, and VHS TAPES documents the abuse before the structures burn.
Posters of HULK HOGAN, JACKSON, and KEN GRIFFEY JR hint at curated normalcy for the children.

### Grounds and Structures

#### Locations
- CORNUCOPIA HOUSE
  - Rural foster compound near ZION MD targeted for the midnight assault.
- HOUSE
  - Main residence with boarded windows, hidden bunkrooms, and filthy common rooms.
- BARN
  - Former dairy structure turned cage stack for neglected animals and kids' pets.
- SHED
  - Outbuilding stuffed with VHS TAPES, recording gear, and the SMALL BLACK BOOK.
- COTTAGE
  - Cozy façade concealing the OLD WOMAN and the slug horror that ends in fire.
- BATHROOM

- FAMILY ROOM

  - Wooded opening holding every structure and providing cover for surveillance.

## Aftermath

### Summary
CLOVE demands the team kill all the children to prevent spread of what they learned, causing LOTTIE to break ranks and save them instead.
SEAMUS dies from LOTTIE's friendly fire but resurrects with memories of vultures and rotting meat.
DANTE, EDDIE, GWEN, and GRENADINE react to the unexplained injuries and absences while PRASAD notices ERASTUS's frayed nerves.

### Program Fallout

#### Characters
- CLOVE
  - Handler who orders LOTTIE to erase witnesses once the house burns.

### Home Fallout

#### Characters
- DANTE
  - Teen son of LOTTIE whose car-theft history colors the post-mission arguments.
- EDDIE
  - Suspicious husband furious at LOTTIE's disappearances and evasions.
- GRENADINE
  - Daughter who notices the rot stench clinging to SEAMUS after the raid.
- GWEN
  - SEAMUS's spouse pressing for honesty about the gunshot wound and covert travel.
- PRASAD
  - German-raised coworker sparring with ERASTUS during the frayed post-raid shift.

## 2001-2016 Interlude

### Summary
In 2004, all three agents simultaneously encounter SREDNI VASHTAR by SAKI with rotting meat smells and animal messengers.
ERASTUS commits suicide in 2005, leaving LOTTIE and SEAMUS to continue small Program jobs.

### The Book Appearances - 2004

#### Objects
- SREDNI VASHTAR
  - Short story by SAKI (Hector Hugh Monroe) from 1911 that appears to all three agents on the same day:
    - LOTTIE follows a blood trail to a dying bat on the book in a grocery store
    - ERASTUS hits a deer and finds the book on his car
    - SEAMUS's daughter GRENADINE receives it from a crow at a sleepover

### Final Fates

#### Characters
- ERASTUS
  - Commits suicide in his backyard in 2005, unable to cope with the events.
