# 2016

## Zion Investigation - August 2016

### Summary

VELASQUEZ leads WORKING GROUP MASTICATE through MD CORRECTION 3 where 56 child bodies, 11 mummies, and black pudding defy explanation.
ELLE GABLE's fingerprints tie the perps to 2001 Cornucopia House while HOUGHTON's crucifixion with KIM SUN HOO's stolen tools reveals targeted revenge.
OWEN's contraband theft forces CDC removal of evidence as HOGAN faces cameras and TOOMEY pushes for answers.
