# GLENDA

## Basic Info

- Elderly resident of MEYERS POINT TRAILER PARK
- Lives in Florida

## Dialog

### Session 8 – Trailer 19 Interview

> GLENDA answers the knock with "hello? is this because of all the noise I've been making with my birds?" then lists her cockatiel and several budgies staring at her while she sleeps.
> 
> - Tells MO and LOTTIE that her "cats were full of love" and could never have eaten KILLIAN, even though deputies told her otherwise.
> - Blames "nasty drug addict mexicans" for stealing the cats, distinguishes them from KILLIAN's "gentleman callers," and insists not even Crystal would "sleep with a mexican."

## Events and Notes

### Stolen Cats - August 8-15, 2016

- Between 8th-15th August 2016, repeatedly calls sheriff to report stolen cats
- Calls prompt dispatch of ECKSTEIN to MEYERS POINT TRAILER PARK
- ECKSTEIN finds KILLIAN's body during this dispatch

## Significance

- Her reports of stolen cats led to the discovery of KILLIAN's body
- The discovery of KILLIAN's body led to the paper with the three agents' names
- This triggered the entire investigation that led back to CORNUCOPIA HOUSE and the discovery of 56 bodies
- Stolen cats may be connected to unnatural elements (pattern of animal involvement throughout the case)
- Provides ongoing intel during the Florida follow-up by confirming the cats all vanished the same day and that Crystal entertained a constant rotation of men in trailer 19.
