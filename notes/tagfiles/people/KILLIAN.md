# Crystal KILLIAN (codename "Clove")

## Basic Info

- Florida shut-in
- Elderly resident
- Lived in MEYERS POINT TRAILER PARK
- Library assistant at FLORIDA SOUTHWESTERN STATE UNIVERSITY
- Operated under the Delta Green codename "Clove" during the 2001 CORNUCOPIA HOUSE operation
- born 1982
- Found dead on August 15, 2016

## Dialog

No direct dialog recorded.

## Events and Notes

### 2001 - The Operation

- Met with LOTTIE at the HOLIDAY INN to brief her on the CORNUCOPIA HOUSE raid.
- Organized the operation, provided the beeper number, and acted as LOTTIE's handler under the name "Clove."
- After the cottage burned and YELENA was killed, answered LOTTIE's call and ordered her to destroy evidence, keep the Program in the dark, and "do what you have to do"—implying the children should be killed.

### LOTTIE's Reaction

- LOTTIE relays the call to SEAMUS and ERASTUS, saying: "ok i'm gonna be straight with you: before we met at the HOLIDAY INN, I met with this agent KILLIAN — codename 'Clove' — from a program called Delta Green ... and she just told me to kill all those fucking kids. I think she's fucking cuckoo bananas." 

### August 2016

- GLENDA (elderly resident of MEYERS POINT TRAILER PARK) repeatedly calls sheriff between 8th-15th Aug to report stolen cats
- Monday 15 Aug: ECKSTEIN dispatched to drive through MEYERS POINT TRAILER PARK
- ECKSTEIN notices graffiti, kicked-in door
- Finds decaying body of KILLIAN
- ECKSTEIN calls it in

### Evidence Found

- EMT finds bloody piece of paper with three names on it:
  - Charlotte Watkins
  - SEAMUS McGillicutty
  - ERASTUS Boudreau
- Plus the address of CORNUCOPIA HOUSE (now a jail)

### Investigation

- Wed 17 Aug: ECKSTEIN calls Maryland PD, asks about CORNUCOPIA HOUSE
- Mentions agents' names
- Questions are in pursuit of homicide
- This trips DG voice recognition thing
- Flags the finding as high-priority
- FBI (program) personnel from MIAMI meet with ECKSTEIN offering services
- Really the point is to question ECKSTEIN about program operatives
- The FBI delegates from MIAMI discover that ECKSTEIN has already closed down the investigation as natural causes (which is suspicious)

### Session 8 – Florida Follow-Up

- Sheriff DEACON BENSON releases KILLIAN's case files to the agents so they can review ECKSTEIN's photos of clawed walls, ceiling-to-floor arterial spray, and the graffiti DOODLES painted above her trailer door.
- Inside the taped-off trailer, LOTTIE recognizes KILLIAN from a 2001 gas-station encounter during which she used the codename "Clove," confirming the alias.
- Library supervisor CORDUROY recalls KILLIAN as a brilliant yet deeply sad $8/hour assistant who taught herself Mandarin, researched "weird religions," and kept a private carrel full of Burmese ghost stories and Egyptian blood-rites texts.
- Bar regulars at JO MAMAS SWAMP SHACK refer to her as "Deep Throat Crystal," describing her string of hookups (BOBBY and CLAYTON) and the sorrow that hung over her even while doing drugs and taking men home.
