# SAMUEL BALL

## Basic Info
- Survivor of CORNUCOPIA HOUSE
- Adopted by CLARENCE MCLEOD and BECKY MARSHALL (then Becky McLeod) in 2002 at age 6
- Returned to state custody in 2005 after violent incidents

## Session 6 – Archive Dossier and Testimony
> STATE ARCHIVE files flag SAMUEL BALL as "unofficially and illegally" returned by the MCLEODs. BECKY MARSHALL recounts SAMUEL BALL calmly attempting to stab ANNE MCLEOD with a kitchen knife, constant screaming fits, and repeated crow visitations at his bedroom window.

> In 2009 BECKY MARSHALL finds SAMUEL BALL among a silent group of teens at ST LAWRENCE CHURCH in MIAMI; he smiles for the first time as he stabs her eight times with an icepick before vanishing.
