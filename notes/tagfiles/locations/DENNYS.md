# DENNYS

## Basic Info
- Chain diner used as neutral meeting spot near ZION MD
- Site of the planned breakfast meeting between LOTTIE and COSTA on Sunday morning

## Session 6 – Blackmail Breakfast
> 5AM: SHELF privately goes to DENNYS and scopes around to find a good parking spot, entrances, exits, etc; returns to the HOLIDAY INN. We park the SUV at 7:20, notice two suspicious cars, and stage LOTTIE's solo entrance while SEAMUS and SHELF monitor from outside.

> Inside DENNYS, COSTA arrives in sunglasses and a trenchcoat, demands to be called "Nightlord," and attempts to blackmail LOTTIE over breakfast orders ("moons over my hammy" and a short stack). He insists she kill SARAH COSTA and frame PABLO RUIZ, threatening to expose her cover if the job isn't done within 48 hours.

> After LOTTIE excuses herself to the bathroom, SHELF and SEAMUS execute Plan B in the parking lot, abducting COSTA as he exits DENNYS and loading him into the SUV while LOTTIE collects the food in a doggie bag.
