# GARRETT COUNTY PRISON

## Session 6 – Interview with BECKY MARSHALL
> We travel to GARRETT COUNTY PRISON to interview BECKY MARSHALL, who was serving time for cheque fraud and reckless abandonment after returning SAMUEL BALL to the state. In the visitation room she recounts SAMUEL BALL's escalating violence, the attempted stabbing of her daughter ANNE MCLEOD, and the later icepick attack that occurred after she tracked him to ST LAWRENCE CHURCH.
