# MIAMI

## Session 6 – ST LAWRENCE CHURCH Encounter
> BECKY MARSHALL recounts traveling to MIAMI in 2009 after losing custody of ANNE MCLEOD. She finds SAMUEL BALL living with a silent, smiling group of kids at the ST LAWRENCE CHURCH homeless shelter. SAMUEL BALL greets her with a smile and stabs her eight times with an icepick before disappearing back into the city.
