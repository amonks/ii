# CERISE AND CLYDE

## Basic Info

- Pair of Program-adjacent operatives cited in Crystal KILLIAN's journal
- Worked with KILLIAN until both died together in a garage shootout circa early 2000

## Session 8 – Journal Entry

> During the evidence review the agents note that the earliest portion of KILLIAN's journal contains coded notes about working with CERISE AND CLYDE before they were killed, underscoring her long history with covert operations.
