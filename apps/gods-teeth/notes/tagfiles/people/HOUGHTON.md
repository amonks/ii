### Autopsy Findings (performed by SEAMUS)
- Age 40 at time of death
- Died at 3am, found at 7pm the same day
- Body is remarkably well preserved; would expect decomp but none
- 3 puncture wounds (palms and heart)
- All teeth removed
- Lower jaw hanging off
- Eyeballs appear to have burst from pressure from jaw
- Cause of death: shock and asphyxiation (on her own blood)
- Heavy bruising about the head and torso; more beating than shown in video
- Scrapes on top of feet
- Jaw severed with matic
- Rip between 2nd and 3rd metacarpals indicating struggle against the screwdriver
- Brain has serious glial scarring; consistent with late-term dementia or cte
  - An amount of deep brain damage that looks like a linebacker or an alzheimer; astonishing that she was functional at all in life
