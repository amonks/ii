# MEYERS POINT TRAILER PARK

## Basic Info

- Trailer park on the edge of the Okaloacoochee Slough State Forest in Florida
- Home to GLENDA and the late Crystal KILLIAN
- Previously spelled "Meyers" in older notes

## Session 8 – Return Visit

> The team weaves past the park manager's office, is told GLENDA still occupies trailer 19, and notes how the lot backs directly into cypress swamp.
>
> - GLENDA describes nights spent under the gaze of her birds, swears her vanished cats were "full of love," and blames "drug addict mexicans" for their disappearance.
> - The agents find KILLIAN's trailer taped off, furniture piled outside, bear claw marks, and DOODLES graffiti over the door before confirming the steep drop into the marsh behind the singlewide.
